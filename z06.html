Modern Horsepower

2018 Chevy Corvette Z06

Not only does the new corvette look better than ever it is also packing a major punch in performance. The new Z06 comes with a Supercharged 6.2L V8 is cranking out 650 horsepower and 650ft lbs of torque. What that means for the driver of this car is to expect a 0 - 60 time in under 3 seconds , which is incredible. To get to that 0-60 time drivers can either choose one of two transmissions , either a 7 speed manual or a 8 speed paddle shit automatic .This corvette has a top speed near 200 Mph.
