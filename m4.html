Modern Horsepower

2018 BMW M4

Not only does BMW make luxury vehicles, but they also focus on performance with some models.The new M4 comes with a 3.0 inline Six cylinder turbocharged motor that is cranking out 425 horsepower and 406ft lbs of torque. What that means for the driver of this car is to expect a 0 - 60 time in under 4 seconds , which is incredible. To get to that 0-60 time drivers can either choose one of two transmissions , either a 6 speed manual or a 7 speed paddle shit automatic. This Bmw will reach a top speed of 155 Mph.
